Spartanburg County Sheriff

Request: All contracts, licenses, MOUs, MOAs, data agreements, letters of intent, State of Work documents, Scope of Work documents, and other agreement documents with Flock Safety or Flock Group that were active any time between 1/1/2020 and 10/30/2025.

Results: View the PDF online at MuckRock.com

Overview:

  • Falcon Flex portable LPR cameras qty 2 and software access for $7,000 annually. - pdf page 15
  • Contract effective on 02/22/2024 for 2 years, auto-renews for 2 more years, requires 30 day non-renewal notice. - pages 1 and 28
  • Data retention period for Spartanburg County is 30 days. Flock can collect the data, meaning copy it to another location and retain it indefinitely.- page 15 and 25
  • Flock has the right to use our (anonymized) data to train their AI. - page 25
  • Flock is not liable for any acts by county staff. If someone wins a lawsuit related to the misuse of the Flock system, taxpayers will foot the bill. - pages 3 and 22
  • Customers own the data, and Flock has a license to use it. Flock cannot sell the data. - page 24
  • Flock can disclose our data to other agencies or third parties. This brings into question the meaning of the county "owning" the data. If the county doesn't have full control of the data, then do they really own it? - page 27, page 4 sec. 6.10
  • Flock can push updates to software or devices, with no language restricting the addition of new features. - page 22
  • Flock allows non-sworn county employees to have Flock accounts. - page 24
  • If Flock devices are damaged, taxpayers will foot the bill for replacement. - page 29
